About This Home

Charming 1910 Bungalow with Character

Step into the warmth of this well-loved bungalow on Church Street — a home that wears its 115-year history proudly while offering practical, everyday comfort. High ceilings and a bright, open layout fill every room with natural light, creating an inviting feel the moment you walk through the door.

The kitchen flows into a dedicated eat-in dining area and connects directly to a full laundry room, adding the kind of convenience that makes daily life easy. The tall attic offers genuine potential as a loft, art studio, home office, or extra sleeping space.

Outside, a covered front porch sets the tone for quiet mornings, while the rear deck opens onto a fully fenced yard with large mature trees — perfect for pets, gardening, or weekend cookouts. A carport and a large detached workshop/shed round out the lot. The red matching shed in the backyard is a charming touch that ties everything together.

Availability note: The home is currently occupied and will be available after July 10, 2026. Viewings can be arranged by appointment.
